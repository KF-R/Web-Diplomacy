### Order Sheet ###

*Note that by this order sheet convention, sea regions AND south coast positions for Bulgaria (Bul), Spain (Spa) and St. Petersburg (Stp) are fully capitalized. If the normally (first character) capitalized version of the three letter abbreviation is used, north/east coast is assumed.*
##
***Example:***
```
Spring 1901
Austria: A Vie–Tri, A Bud–Gal, F Tri–Alb
England: A Lvp–Yor, F Lon–NTH, F Edi–NWG
France: A Par–Bur, A Mar–Spa, F Bre–Pic
Germany: A Ber–Kie, A Mun–Ruh, F Kie–Den
Italy: A Ven–Pie, A Rom–Ven, F Nap–ION
Russia: A Mos–Ukr, A War–Gal, F STP–BOT, F Sev–BLA
Turkey: A Con–Bul, A Smy–Con, F Ank–BLA
```
